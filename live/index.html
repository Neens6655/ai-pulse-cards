June was the month the AI race became a sovereignty race. Washington asked OpenAI to wait on its next model, Anthropic overtook OpenAI to become the world's most valuable private lab on its way to an IPO, and Google's brain trust walked out the door. Beneath the drama, two quieter shifts matter more for the Gulf: the price of frontier AI stopped falling, and compute — not models — became the asset worth owning. Abu Dhabi made that bet early.
